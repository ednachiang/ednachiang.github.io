A Microbial World: Surviving Hibernation

May 4, 2019

I had the pleasure of creating a science comic about my hibernation microbiology research in collaboration with JKX Comics and Dr. Jaye Gardiner in their project Gaining STEAM!

The goals of Gaining STEAM! (science, technology, engineering, art and math) are to use art as a medium to make science more accessible and highlight a diversity of researchers-- both in terms of research and scientists as individuals.

My comic tells the story of two microbes and their quest for survival in a hibernating squirrel.
